About

I was born in a sleepy town in Guimba Nueva Ecija. When I was young, I studied at the Our Lady of the Sacred Heart Academy until the 6th grade. I joined a lot of organizations like the Boy Scouts of the Philippines, The Drum and Bugle Lyre and even became a member of the Knights of the Altar.

High school was a bit different. I have to travel from our town all the way to Tarlac City. I was fortunate enough to study at Don Bosco Technical Institute. Gained a lot of friends, was able to develop a lot of skillsets and equipped myself with a lot of experiences, both learned inside and outside the school. Later on, during my third and fourth year, my parents finally decided to let me stay with my uncle.




Took up Bachelor of Science in Information and Computing Science in this University which became the only University granted by the Commision on Higher Education as Center for Excellence.
